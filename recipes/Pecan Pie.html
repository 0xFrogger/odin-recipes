Pecan Pie

Description

There are few things quite as comforting as a sweet, rich, nutty pecan pie. Looking for the best pecan pie recipe ever? The Allrecipes community can't get enough of this top-rated recipe â€” it's simple, delicious, and it comes together in under an hour with basic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Corn Syrup
  • Butter
  • Cornstarch and Water
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Vanilla
  • Pecans
  • Pie Shell

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Combine sugar, corn syrup, butter, water, and cornstarch in a saucepan over medium heat. Bring to a full boil; remove from heat
  3. Beat eggs in a large bowl with mixer until frothy. Gradually beat in cooked syrup mixture. Stir in salt, vanilla, and pecans
  4. Pour pecan mixture into pie shell
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until filling is set, 45 to 50 minutes
  6. Enjoy
